UZURI TOWN by Synclair Lavendel
First created in 2012, UZURI is a prim-built sim with very little mesh and few updates. A truly historical build using mainly the tools found in the viwer. Consisting of a Town Hall, Nightclub, Old Town Town Hall Building based upon those in Synclair's home town. Also features a fully furnished Pirate Radio Station based upon the ship Mebo II, Radio Northsea International, a Freethought Conference Centre, basic castle and a local Pub.
