ARL/STTC, in cooperation with UCF/Institute for Simulation Technology, developed a content ingestion method to allow for actual operational areas to be scanned using LIDAR and then produce scenario output for use in a virtual world. The initial proof of concept of this workflow was to use the UCF Partnership II and Partnership III buildings and surrounding areas
