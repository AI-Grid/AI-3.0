Haxor Outpost 78-369
By Ener Hax http://enerhax.com/oars/haxoroutpost78.html

An easy to modify base colony for yourself or even commercial use. the city centre is a mall ready for shops, stores, and clubs. four condominium pods could be set up for four friends on a Kitely region!

relax by the Singapore Garden by the Bay inspired trees or swim in the pool


specs
format: unzipped OAR - 1 mb
prims: 900
textures: default terrains, wood, and water
scripts: none
