JOOMLA/JOPENSIM UPDATE


IMPORTANT: this version required changes in DreamGrid, Joomla and in jOpensim.  This also the reason why this version will not update as usual with the Joomla updater. Clicking on update in Joomla without following these instructions will result in a broken jOpensim installation.  If you also have jOpenSim PayPal in use, you will need to update this component as well.

How to Update:

First, update your Joomla installation to version 3.9.23.  This can be done by logging into the Administrator panel and clicking the notice. Or click on Components->Joomla Update.  Do not update past Version 3.9.23 to V 4!

In DreamGrid, go to Setup->Settings-> and then to Joomla/jOpensim.

Press the [Update] button to update to the latest jOpensim. This will take you to this screen:

 

Click the big green “Or Browse for file” and navigate to the Outworldzfiles\jOpensimfiles\ folder.  Select “pkg_jopensim.0.3.2.0.zip” and click Ok.    This will update you to jOpensim V3.0.2.0.
