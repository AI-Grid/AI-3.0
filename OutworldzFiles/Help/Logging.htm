Logging

There are multiple levels of logging in Opensim:

"All"       - Everything is logged. This includes every level below.

"DEBUG"              - Developer level notices are sent to the log, plus the levels below.

"INFO" - Useful information, plus the levels below.

"WARN"               - Warnings are logged, plus the levels below.

"ERROR"               - Errors are logged, plus the levels below.

"FATAL"                - Only Fatal errors are logged.

"OFF"    - nothing is sent to the log files.

"Logs" are the Robust.Log located in Opensim\bin, and the Opensim logs located in Opensim\Bin\Regions in each DOS Box folder.
