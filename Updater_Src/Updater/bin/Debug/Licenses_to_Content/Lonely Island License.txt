Description:   A contribution for the June Challenge of OpenSim Creations (http://opensim-creations.com/2013/06/04/june-challenge-the-great-outdoors/). 
               Shipwrecked on a small island with only some coconuts to feed yourself, not the idea about spending your vacation.
			   Textures are all own or from Open Domain sources. The object is created in an OpenSimulator Virtual 3D World (OpenSim 0.7.5 RC1) 
			   and exported with Cool VL Viewer (v1.26.6) No scripts are included.
			   If you use it in your own environment, please sent an image to me.
